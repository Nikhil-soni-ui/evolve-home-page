About Us

Evolve is an interdisciplinary technical club initiated by the Department of Electrical Engineering at the National Institute of Technology, Bhopal. Founded in 2023, Evolve focuses on providing practical hands-on experience in various project categories and aims to build newer generations of Electric Vehicles through workshops, project proposals, and other methods.

Our Specialties

Electric Vehicles, Power Electronics, Hybrid Propulsion Systems, Power Systems, Machine Learning, Microcontrollers, and Microprocessors.
